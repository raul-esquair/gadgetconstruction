## Campaign Structure

Two campaigns split by buyer behavior — urgent repairs close fast, planned projects need nurturing.

### Campaign 2: Planned Projects

Higher ticket size, longer sales cycle. These buyers are comparing contractors.

## 30-Day Optimization Plan

**Week 1:** Launch campaigns, monitor daily. Check search terms report for irrelevant queries and add negatives.

**Week 2:** Pause keywords with clicks but zero conversions. Increase bids on converting keywords.

**Week 3:** Test ad copy variations. A/B test headlines on top-performing ad groups.

**Week 4:** Review full month data. Reallocate budget from underperforming ad groups to winners. Consider switching to Target CPA bidding if you have 15+ conversions.
